Barrage.js --A User-friendly Tool for Designing WebApps

Hi, this is the library for creating "Barrage".

i
If you want to change comment height or width, you need to terminate your current barrage first. Click "change" to restart the barrage.
i
The comment size and color here is customized for all comment texts in Barrage. To change size or color for a spefic comment, please see example 2 on "Examples".
i
"Pool Number" is the maximum number of comments in one row of Barrage.
i
"Channel Number" is the maximum number of rows of your Barrage.
i
"Sent Delay Number" is a factor you want to delay your comment to be sent out.
i
"Sent Speend Number" is number of seconds you want to your comment to complete the transition on your display region.
i
If you want to change direction or animation, you need to terminate your current barrage first. Click "change" to restart the barrage.
i
"Recurrence Status" is whether you want your comment to be sent repeatedly, the barrage only sent a comment once if "recurStatus" is "false".
i
Adding hover effect to comment nodes. If you want to change comment hover effect, you need to terminate your current barrage first. Click "change" to restart the barrage.
